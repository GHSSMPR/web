A Good education is a foundation for a better future

“Come to Learn, Go to Serve” is the motto of GHSS Mahadevpur. Our mission is to impart value oriented quality education with emphasis on national integration to develop children as good human beings with all round development. Quality education calls for involvement of teachers, students, parents and the community in our mission and we are committed to continually improving our quality education to satisfy our children.

GHSS Mahadevpur an ongoing journey

Under the Directorate of School education, Govt. of Arunachal Pradesh, GHSS Mahadevpur begins its journey as a Primary School in year 1956 and subsequently upgraded to Middle School in year 1967, as Secondary School in year 1978 and as Higher Secondary School in year 1988. Now it is CBSE affiliated English medium, Coeducational School from Class IX to XII having Science and Humanity Stream in senior secondary level.
